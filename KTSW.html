Kimchi Tuna Salad

★ ★ ★ ★ ★
TOTAL TIME: 10 minutes YIELD: 10 minutes
Creamy, crunchy, tangy! It’s a Kimchi Tuna Salad Wrap! Packed
with tuna, kewpie mayo, kimchi, avocado, cucumbers, and a bit of
chili crisp – all snuggled into a wrap. So, so good!
